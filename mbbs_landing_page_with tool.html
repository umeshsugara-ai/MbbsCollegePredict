FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is an MBBS abroad valid in India?
+
Yes, subject to prevailing regulations and licensing pathways such as NMC norms and required exams (NExT). Vidysea only partners with NMC-listed universities.
Can I get MBBS admission with a low NEET score?
+
Yes, depending on your score, budget, and seat availability in India or abroad. Many reputed universities accept a range of qualifying NEET scores. We help match you with the best-fit options.
Is MBBS abroad safe for Indian students?
+
Yes, when you choose verified universities in the right countries. Vidysea only recommends institutions in countries with established Indian student communities and strong support networks.
What is cheaper — India private or abroad?
+
It depends on the college and country. In many cases, abroad can be more affordable than Indian private and deemed university fees. We provide full cost comparisons for every option.
Do you help parents understand costs clearly?
+
Yes. We provide transparent, itemised cost planning including tuition, hostel, travel, visa, and miscellaneous — so families can plan with complete financial clarity from Day 1.
What are PG and job opportunities after MBBS abroad?
+
After clearing NExT/FMGE, you can appear for NEET-PG in India just like any domestic MBBS graduate. We provide career roadmap guidance during and after your MBBS to plan ahead.
